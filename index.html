Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply choose a ready-made box or design a custom one, then click the "Order via WhatsApp" button. We'll finalize everything with you in the chat!

What are the payment options?

We accept various payment methods via WhatsApp, including bank transfer, mobile wallets (like Vodafone Cash), and cash on delivery in some areas. We'll provide options in our chat.

Do you deliver all over Egypt?

Yes! We deliver across Egypt. Delivery times and fees vary depending on the governorate. We'll confirm the details with you when you order.

What is your cancellation policy?

For ready-made boxes, you can cancel up to 12 hours before the scheduled delivery. For custom boxes, cancellations are possible before we begin production. Please chat with us as soon as possible if you need to make a change.

Are my personal messages and photos kept private?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All messages, photos, and personal details shared with us for your custom gift are kept strictly confidential and are only used to create your order.
